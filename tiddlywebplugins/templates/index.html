News

Avox is pleased to announce that in May 2012 www.wiki-data.com will be succeeded by www.AvoxData.com (AvoxData).

AvoxData is Avox's new web portal. It will present all the content currently available on Wiki-Data and will have a significantly upgraded subscription-based service that will offer enhanced features:

  • Instant access to the complete Avox standard field set for subscribed Legal Entities
  • Export Legal Entity details to PDF or csv
  • Track your Challenges and new Entity Registrations
  • Filter search results using Faceted Search
  • Monitor entities of interest for changes
  • Inclusion of client specific identifiers linked to AVID's and, once available, Legal Entity Identifiers*

www.wiki-data.com will remain available to users after the launch of www.AvoxData.com.

If you are a user of the wiki-data API please contact us to learn about additional AvoxData developments including a new API.

To learn more about the new service please contact avoxdata@avox.info

*provisional legal entity identifiers are already available on www.wiki-data.com
